World Cup News

The biggest scorline in the history of the FIFA World Cup qualifires - and indeed in the history of international football - was recorded on 11 April 2001, when Australia beat America Samoa 31-0.

The legendary match also brought global renown for Archie Thompson, whose 13 goal haul set a new world record, which stands to this day, for an individual player in a single international match.

And though the defeat earned the American Samoa ignominy, so inspiring has their subsequent recovery been that it is not the subject of an acclaimed documentary, 'Next Goal Wins', showing across the world.
